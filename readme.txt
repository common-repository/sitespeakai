== Description ==

SiteSpeakAI lets you create a ChatGPT chatbot that is custom trained on your website or business data. Your chatbot can be embedded on your website to answer your visitor questions about your products and services in realtime, 24/7.

With this plugin, you can easily embed your SiteSpeakAI chatbot into your Wordpress website.

== 3rd Party Services ==

This plugin uses the 3rd party service, SiteSpeakAI (https://sitespeak.ai), to embed a chatbot on your site. The terms and conditions for SiteSpeakAI is available here: https://sitespeak.ai/terms-of-service and the privacy policy: https://sitespeak.ai/privacy-policy.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I install my chatbot? =

With this plugin, you can set the chatbot ID (found in the settings page for your chatbot on https://sitespeak.ai/dashboard), and pasting it in the textbox.
